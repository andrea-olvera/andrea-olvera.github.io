ML-Driven Trading Strategy and Portfolio Analysis

Applied machine learning techniques in Excel to backtest two algorithmic trading strategies, Golden Cross and Exponential Moving Average (EMA), on NVDA and JPM using historical data from 1999 to 2025. Constructed a value and growth portfolio and used Excel's FORECAST.LINEAR function to project portfolio performance over 12, 24, and 60 months, concluding that the value-weighted portfolio outperformed the growth portfolio across all forecast horizons.
